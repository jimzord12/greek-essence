## 11. Short Contact Form and Direct Contact

If C-26 is included, it uses a separate, smaller schema: name, email, optional approved phone, message/category, required privacy acknowledgement, and optional marketing consent. It posts to the same handler with a `formType` discriminator or a separate route with the same email pattern. It must not force trip qualification questions into a general enquiry.

Email, phone, WhatsApp, consultation, and Viber actions remain content/config-driven. Render only channels that are approved and monitored. External consultation embeds are deferred; a clear outbound link is sufficient if the scheduling path is chosen. Each action has a visible label, safe `tel:`/`mailto:`/external URL behavior, and later analytics hook point.

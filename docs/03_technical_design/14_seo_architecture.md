## 14. SEO Architecture

- Render all meaningful content as static HTML, not client-only content.
- Generate unique localized titles, descriptions, canonical URLs, Open Graph/Twitter fields, social images, and `hreflang` alternates.
- Generate `sitemap.xml` and `robots.txt` from the validated route/content registry.
- Use real anchor links and contextual internal relationships between destinations, experiences, journeys, FAQ, trust, and CTA routes.
- Generate conservative structured data from verified content only: `Organization`, `WebSite`, `BreadcrumbList`, `ItemList`, `AboutPage`, `ContactPage`, and eligible destination/journey types after content review.
- Do not emit ratings, reviews, offers, prices, availability, or booking schema.
- Keep image alt text and dimensions correct. Avoid thin, duplicate, placeholder, and untranslated pages.
- Redirect any changed public slug in one hop; do not ship broken internal links.

The prototype provides SEO-ready architecture, not production SEO evidence. Search Console, keyword measurement, analytics, content publishing cadence, and production indexing controls are later work.

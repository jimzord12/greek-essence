## 28. Change Control and Approval

Changes to product scope, public claims, page/interaction behavior, form fields, or production acceptance criteria first update the PRD or Prototype Specification. Technical changes update this document and implementation together. The client prototype may evolve quickly, but deviations from the governing experience must be recorded rather than introduced informally.

## 18. Review and Change Control

Before approval, review this specification against the PRD page inventory, component inventory, form requirements, functional/non-functional requirements, open decisions, and MVP acceptance criteria.

Any proposed change must identify whether it is:

- a prototype clarification within this document’s authority;
- a product change requiring an update to the PRD;
- an implementation choice belonging in the Technical Design;
- a business, content, operational, or legal decision requiring the appropriate owner.

The specification is approved only when product intent is unchanged, provisional claims remain visibly controlled, and the prototype acceptance criteria are testable.

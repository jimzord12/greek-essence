## 34. Plan My Trip

- Reduce unrelated imagery and navigation noise.
- Use a narrow 720–880 px form container.
- Use a subtle surface shell only if it improves grouping.
- Step title and progress are visually stable.
- Continue/Back hierarchy is consistent.
- Errors remain close to fields and summarized above.
- On mobile, actions may remain near the viewport bottom only when they do not obscure content or errors.
- The saved-draft notice is visible but not alarming.

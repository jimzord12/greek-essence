## 4. Confirmed, Provisional, and Open Decisions

### 4.1 Confirmed design decisions

The following decisions were explicitly approved during design discovery:

1. Cormorant Garamond for display/editorial roles.
2. Inter for interface and body roles.
3. Aegean Editorial color direction.
4. Soft editorial corner radii.
5. Minimal natural shadows.
6. Generous editorial density.
7. Solid deep-Aegean primary CTA.
8. Lucide iconography.
9. Light appearance only.
10. Cinematic editorial hero treatment.
11. Predominantly warm-ivory page canvas.
12. Transparent-to-solid header transition.
13. Subtle Mediterranean decorative details.
14. Soft low-contrast borders.

### 4.2 Provisional design values

Exact color values, type-scale values, spacing values, shadow formulas, breakpoints, and decorative assets are implementation-ready recommendations but remain subject to visual review with the final logo, imagery, and representative bilingual content.

They may be tuned without changing the design direction, provided:

- semantic roles remain stable;
- accessibility is not weakened;
- changes are recorded in this document;
- implementation and documentation are updated together.

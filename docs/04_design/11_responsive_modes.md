## 11. Responsive Modes

| Mode | Reference range | Primary characteristics | Density rule |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Compact | 320–767 px | single column, mobile menu, vertical routes, full-width form controls | generous but efficient |
| Medium | 768–1199 px | 2-column collections, selected split editorial sections | preserve readable measure |
| Wide | 1200 px+ | full navigation, 3–4 card collections, immersive media | generous editorial rhythm |

### 11.1 Required checks

- 320 px width
- 390 × 844
- 834 × 1112
- 1440 × 1024
- intermediate widths
- landscape mobile/tablet
- 200% zoom
- English and Greek
- reduced motion
- forced colors where supported

### 11.2 Content-density rules

- Opening screens contain one proposition, one supporting thought, and one primary action.
- Card summaries remain concise and decision-useful.
- Do not add horizontal carousels merely to fit more cards.
- Collections may show fewer items before a “View all” path.
- Mobile section spacing should remain visually premium without producing needless empty screens.
- Form fields remain in one logical sequence; two-column field rows are reserved for clearly related short fields on wider screens.

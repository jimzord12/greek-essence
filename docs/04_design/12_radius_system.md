
# Part VI — Shape, Surface, Border, and Elevation

## 12. Radius System

| Token | Value | Usage |
|---|---:|---|
| `radius-xs` | 4 px | small tags, compact status |
| `radius-sm` | 8 px | small controls, tooltips |
| `radius-md` | 10 px | buttons, inputs |
| `radius-lg` | 12 px | dropdowns, compact panels |
| `radius-xl` | 16 px | standard cards and images |
| `radius-2xl` | 20 px | hero/editorial images, dialogs |
| `radius-3xl` | 24 px | rare immersive media |
| `radius-full` | 9999 px | chips, avatar circles, progress dots |

Rules:

- Roundedness is restrained and consistent.
- Standard cards use 16 px.
- Inputs and buttons use 10 px.
- Pills are limited to choice chips, compact status, and appropriate tags.
- Do not mix multiple radii within one component without an intentional nested-surface reason.
- Hero media may use square/full-bleed edges when it meets the viewport or page edge.

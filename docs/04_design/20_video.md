## 20. Video

Video is optional.

Rules:

- still imagery is the default hero medium;
- no autoplay with sound;
- persistent motion requires a pause control;
- use poster-first loading;
- do not block LCP or primary content;
- provide captions/transcripts where meaningful;
- respect reduced-motion;
- do not use video to compensate for weak content;
- mobile may use a still image instead;
- avoid background-video loops longer than necessary.

## 29. Home Visual Guidance

- Home receives the strongest cinematic hero.
- The first supporting section should reduce visual intensity and establish trust through clear copy.
- Featured destinations use large image-led cards, with Paros & Antiparos allowed strategic emphasis.
- Avoid four identical equal-weight blocks above the fold.
- Use one subtle Mediterranean divider/motif at most in the opening half.
- How It Works should feel ordered and human, not like a SaaS onboarding timeline.
- Trust section should use portraits/narrative before logos.
- Final CTA may use a deep-blue surface to provide a clear conclusion.

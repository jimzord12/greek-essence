# Part IV — Product Scope and Requirements

## 21. MVP Scope

### 21.1 Included Product Capabilities

The MVP must include:

- responsive marketing website;
- English-first bilingual content structure with Greek;
- Home, Destinations, Experiences, Journeys, About, FAQ, Contact, Plan My Trip, and required legal pages;
- priority destination content;
- experience and sample-journey content;
- multi-step custom-trip request;
- reliable form delivery, ownership, and acknowledgement workflow;
- consultation action or integration;
- email, WhatsApp, phone, and approved Viber contact actions;
- SEO-ready content structure and metadata capability;
- conversion measurement requirements;
- privacy and consent capability appropriate to selected tools and markets;
- accessible navigation, content, and forms;
- content and routing foundations that permit future expansion.

### 21.2 MVP Product Characteristics

The MVP must be:

- understandable;
- trustworthy;
- responsive;
- performant;
- search-friendly;
- accessible;
- measurable;
- maintainable;
- operationally reliable.

### 21.3 Validation Purpose

The MVP should answer:

- Can Greek Essence attract relevant visitors?
- Do visitors understand and trust the offer?
- Which destinations and experience themes generate intent?
- Will suitable visitors complete the request journey?
- What lead quality results?
- Can the business respond reliably without excessive manual burden?

### Key Decisions

- MVP completeness is defined by reliable product outcomes, not maximum feature count.
- Marketing, lead handling, and operational follow-up form one end-to-end product journey.

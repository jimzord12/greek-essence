## 11. Offer and Service Definition

### 11.1 Confirmed Core Offers

The MVP may present the following confirmed services:

- tailor-made travel planning in Greece;
- island-hopping experiences;
- authentic local experiences;
- multi-destination Greece itineraries;
- VIP concierge and travel-planning support.

### 11.2 Strongly Supported Experience Themes

Subject to final operational confirmation and content readiness:

- premium travel;
- honeymoons and romantic getaways;
- family travel;
- private excursions and experiences;
- cultural and historical travel;
- gastronomy;
- boutique accommodation and hospitality.

### 11.3 Services Requiring Confirmation

These must not receive prominent navigation or dedicated launch pages until the business confirms that they are active, deliverable, and supportable:

- organized group tours;
- adventure, nature, and hiking;
- cruises and marine tourism;
- MICE, corporate travel, and events.

### 11.4 Service Presentation Rules

Each service description should explain:

- the traveler outcome;
- who the service suits;
- the planning complexity it removes;
- the role of local knowledge or partner access;
- the expected planning relationship;
- the next appropriate action.

Service pages must not promise operational capabilities, inclusions, or support levels that have not been approved.

### Key Decisions

- Confirmed services may be promoted; unconfirmed services remain hidden or secondary.
- Outcomes and planning value take precedence over operational feature lists.

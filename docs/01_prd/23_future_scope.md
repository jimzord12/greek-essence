## 23. Future Scope

Future capabilities may be considered after validation:

### 23.1 Content and Trust Expansion

- original photography and video;
- Greek Essence testimonials;
- traveler stories and case studies;
- travel guides and editorial content;
- expanded destinations and journeys;
- gallery and richer media storytelling;
- newsletter supported by an actual publishing strategy.

### 23.2 Operational Optimization

- CRM integration;
- Ezus or alternative itinerary-platform integration;
- automated lead routing;
- response templates;
- proposal workflow;
- source and lead-quality reporting;
- operational dashboards;
- structured customer communication.

### 23.3 Platform Evolution

- authenticated customer area;
- itinerary access and management;
- booking or payment where commercially justified;
- document exchange;
- trip updates and support tools;
- third-party service integrations;
- internal operational tools.

### 23.4 Future-Scope Rule

Future scope must be justified by validated demand, operational value, user benefit, commercial viability, legal readiness, and maintenance cost.

### Key Decisions

- Future features remain options rather than promises.
- The product should evolve from evidence, not speculation.

## 22. Out of Scope for MVP

Unless explicitly re-approved, the MVP excludes:

- real-time availability;
- direct online booking;
- online payment;
- user registration and authentication;
- customer dashboard;
- dynamic itinerary management;
- custom back-office system;
- automated proposal generation;
- marketplace functionality;
- live hotel or transport inventory;
- price comparison;
- loyalty program;
- native mobile application;
- AI chatbot;
- large-scale editorial publishing operation;
- complex CRM or itinerary-platform coupling;
- unconfirmed services such as group tours, MICE, cruises, or hiking as major launch categories.

### 22.1 Scope Protection Rule

An excluded capability may not be introduced merely because a tool or framework makes it easy. It requires a documented product need, operational readiness, impact assessment, and approval.

### Key Decisions

- The MVP remains a lead-generation and trust-building product.
- Features that imply transaction, availability, or customer account capability are deliberately excluded.

# Part V — Risks, Decisions, and Acceptance

## 27. Dependencies and Content Gaps

The following inputs are required before final design or launch:

- finalized logo files and usage approval;
- approved color and typography direction;
- original or licensed image library;
- final English copy;
- Greek localization;
- founder or team biographies and photography;
- confirmed active service list;
- operational definition of support before and during travel;
- sample itinerary data;
- approved FAQ answers;
- final contact details and channel ownership;
- consultation scheduling process;
- form-handling and lead-ownership workflow;
- response-time commitment;
- evidence and permission for awards, certifications, reviews, and relationships;
- privacy, cookie, and travel terms;
- SEO research and content plan;
- decision on Viber;
- decision on Ezus or alternative future platform timing.

### Dependency Rule

A missing dependency must be visible in planning and acceptance. Placeholder content may be used in prototypes but must not silently become production content.

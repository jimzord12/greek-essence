## 3. Business Context

### 3.1 Business Overview

Greek Essence is a Greece-focused tour operator and travel-planning partner. It creates personalized trips that combine iconic destinations with lesser-known locations, local experiences, carefully selected hospitality, and support before and during travel.

The business relies on human expertise, destination knowledge, hospitality experience, and partner relationships. The website is the digital entry point into that service relationship.

### 3.2 Business Problem

Planning a high-quality multi-destination trip to Greece is difficult for many international travelers. They must evaluate islands and mainland regions, transport connections, seasonal differences, accommodation quality, suitable experiences, and the trade-off between famous destinations and authentic local travel.

Generic travel content provides abundant information but limited confidence. Booking platforms optimize individual transactions rather than the complete trip. Fixed packages reduce choice but often feel impersonal. Greek Essence addresses the gap between self-service research and standardized package travel.

### 3.3 Business Opportunity

There is an opportunity to serve travelers who:

- have sufficient budget but limited planning time;
- value quality, authenticity, and personal service;
- are concerned about expensive mistakes or tourist traps;
- want a coherent multi-stop journey rather than isolated bookings;
- prefer a trusted human expert to a catalogue of options.

### 3.4 Business Model in the MVP

The website generates and qualifies leads. The business then reviews each request, communicates with the traveler, develops a proposal or itinerary, and progresses the relationship manually using the selected operational workflow.

The website itself does not complete a booking or payment in the MVP.

### Key Decisions

- The website initiates a service relationship rather than completing a transaction.
- The planning process and personal expertise must be visible as part of the offer.
- The MVP business workflow may be manual, but it must be reliable and clearly owned.

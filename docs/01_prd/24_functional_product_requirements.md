## 24. Functional Product Requirements

The following requirements define product behavior without prescribing implementation.

### FR-001 — Navigation

Users must be able to navigate the full public site using clear, consistent primary and secondary navigation.

### FR-002 — Language Access

Users must be able to access English and Greek content and understand the current language context.

### FR-003 — Destination Discovery

Users must be able to discover priority and secondary destinations and understand traveler fit and possible combinations.

### FR-004 — Experience Discovery

Users must be able to explore the offer by travel style, need, or experience theme.

### FR-005 — Journey Inspiration

Users must be able to view customizable sample itineraries with route, duration, mood, traveler fit, and representative highlights.

### FR-006 — Brand and Trust

Users must be able to understand who Greek Essence is, why it is credible, and how the planning relationship works.

### FR-007 — Primary Conversion

Users must be able to start and complete a custom-trip request from all relevant page types.

### FR-008 — Secondary Contact

Users must be able to access approved secondary contact methods and a consultation option.

### FR-009 — Form Persistence

The trip-request experience must preserve valid entered data while moving between steps and while handling validation errors.

### FR-010 — Submission Confirmation

A successful request must produce a clear confirmation and explain the next step.

### FR-011 — Request Delivery

Every successful submission must be delivered, stored or recorded, assigned, and acknowledged through the approved operational workflow.

### FR-012 — Consent and Privacy

Personal-data collection must present appropriate information and consent controls.

### FR-013 — Search Discovery

Public content must support indexable, localized, internally linked destination, experience, journey, and brand pages.

### FR-014 — Measurement

The product must support measurement of the agreed conversion funnel and major contact actions.

### FR-015 — Error Communication

Users must receive clear, actionable feedback when input, submission, translation, or content states fail.

### FR-016 — Content Relationships

The product should connect relevant destinations, experiences, and journeys to support exploration.

### FR-017 — Legal Access

Users must be able to access applicable privacy, cookie, and travel terms.

### Key Decisions

- Functional requirements describe user-visible and operational outcomes.
- Implementation choices belong downstream.

## 29. Assumptions

The PRD currently assumes:

- Greek Essence will operate as a genuine tailor-made travel service at launch;
- English and Greek versions are both required;
- the team can manually review and follow up on launch-volume leads;
- approved, credible hospitality background and relationships can be described publicly;
- at least three operationally credible sample journeys can be created;
- the business will define legal and commercial policies before launch;
- a managed service, serverless function, or equivalent operational mechanism can reliably process forms;
- the product owner prefers maintainable, modern, battle-tested implementation choices;
- booking, payment, and authentication are not MVP requirements;
- the owner will approve final brand, content, and service claims.

If an assumption proves false, affected requirements must be reviewed rather than silently worked around.

## 26. Analytics and Product Learning Requirements

### 26.1 Required Product Events

At minimum, measurement should distinguish:

- primary CTA click;
- destination view;
- experience view;
- journey view;
- form start;
- form step progression;
- form validation error;
- form abandonment where measurable and privacy-compliant;
- form submission;
- consultation click;
- email click;
- WhatsApp click;
- phone click;
- Viber click if retained;
- language switch.

### 26.2 Required Dimensions

Where legally and technically appropriate:

- acquisition source and campaign;
- landing page;
- device category;
- language;
- destination interest;
- journey or experience interest;
- form step;
- qualified versus unqualified outcome, initially enriched manually.

### 26.3 Learning Cadence

After launch:

1. verify instrumentation and submission integrity immediately;
2. collect an initial meaningful baseline;
3. review acquisition and funnel quality after approximately 8–12 weeks of meaningful traffic;
4. identify high-exit pages, weak traffic sources, and form friction;
5. prioritize changes using expected business impact and evidence.

### 26.4 Privacy Constraint

Measurement must respect applicable consent and data-protection requirements. The product must not collect unnecessary personal data for analytics.

### Key Decisions

- Analytics exists to improve qualified demand and operations, not merely report traffic.
- Funnel measurement must connect to manually tracked business outcomes.

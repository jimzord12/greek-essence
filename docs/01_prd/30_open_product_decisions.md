## 30. Open Product Decisions

The following questions require business-owner, legal, operational, or content approval. They are not blockers to this PRD draft but may block content production, technical design, or launch.

### 30.1 Launch and Scope

1. What is the approved target launch date?
2. Which optional services are active at launch: group tours, hiking/adventure, cruises, MICE?
3. Which three to six sample journeys are operationally deliverable?

### 30.2 Qualification and Commercial Process

4. What minimum or preferred trip budget indicates a suitable lead?
5. Does the budget field include international flights?
6. Should budget ranges vary by trip duration, party, or service?
7. Will any public `starting from` price guidance be used, or will budget remain form-only?

### 30.3 Lead Operations

8. What response time can the business consistently promise?
9. Who owns incoming leads, and who is the backup?
10. Where are leads stored and tracked initially?
11. Which acknowledgement and notification workflow will be used?
12. Which consultation scheduling tool or process will be used?
13. Is Viber actively monitored and worth retaining?

### 30.4 Integrations and Growth

14. Will Ezus be selected, and at which phase?
15. Which lead-handling interfaces must remain replaceable for future integration?

### 30.5 Trust, Legal, and Content

16. How may Athenaeum Hotels, Porto Bello di Paros, and Althea Resorts be described publicly?
17. Which awards, reviews, certifications, and logos may be used?
18. Who creates and approves English copy and Greek localization?
19. Which photo and video assets exist, and what licensing budget is available?
20. What travel terms, cancellation rules, payment policies, and privacy language apply?
21. Will `greekessence.com` be acquired, redirected, or deferred?

### 30.6 Open-Decision Rule

No contributor may independently settle these questions by assumption when the answer affects public claims, commercial obligations, data handling, or operations.

## 10. Domain Overview

### 10.1 Tailor-Made Travel

A tailor-made journey is created around the traveler's party, dates, budget, pace, interests, accommodation preferences, and desired level of support. Sample journeys may inspire the traveler, but they are not fixed packages unless explicitly stated in the future.

### 10.2 Planning Relationship

The expected high-level process is:

1. The traveler shares trip basics and preferences.
2. Greek Essence reviews the request.
3. A planner contacts the traveler and clarifies needs.
4. Greek Essence prepares a personalized proposal.
5. The proposal is refined and operational details are arranged.
6. The traveler receives support according to the purchased service.

Exact commercial, payment, cancellation, and service terms require business and legal approval.

### 10.3 Journey

In this product, a **Journey** or **Sample Itinerary** is an inspirational, customizable route. It communicates how destinations, duration, and experiences can combine. It must not imply live availability, fixed pricing, or guaranteed inclusions unless those capabilities and commitments actually exist.

### 10.4 Destination

A destination page is not a generic guide. It explains fit, character, combinations, authentic experiences, and the value Greek Essence adds when planning that destination.

### 10.5 Experience

An experience theme helps visitors imagine how they want to travel—such as romance, family, culture, gastronomy, island hopping, or concierge-supported travel—rather than merely where they want to go.

### 10.6 Qualified Lead

A request is qualified when the business has enough information to assess fit and respond efficiently. Minimum qualifying information is expected to include:

- destination or need for guidance;
- dates or flexibility;
- trip duration;
- party composition;
- trip type;
- interests and pace;
- approximate budget;
- valid contact information;
- privacy consent.

### 10.7 Trust Asset

A trust asset is any approved, accurate evidence that reduces perceived risk: founder or team information, hospitality background, partner relationships, certifications, attributed awards, process clarity, future testimonials, privacy reassurance, or operational commitments.

### Key Decisions

- Journeys are customizable inspiration, not packages.
- Domain terms must avoid implying capabilities the business does not yet provide.

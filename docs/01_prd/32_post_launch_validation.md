## 32. Post-Launch Validation

After launch, the team should validate:

- acquisition source quality;
- user understanding of the offer;
- engagement with priority destinations and journeys;
- CTA performance by page type;
- form completion and abandonment;
- lead quality and common rejection reasons;
- response time and operational workload;
- mobile versus desktop conversion;
- English versus Greek usage;
- content opportunities indicated by search and inquiries.

The first optimization cycle should focus on the largest evidenced constraint in the funnel rather than broad redesign.

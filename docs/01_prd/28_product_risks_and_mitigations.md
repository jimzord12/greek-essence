## 28. Product Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Product impact | Required response |
|---|---|---|
| No original visual library | Premium positioning may feel generic | Use strict licensed-media standards and plan original production |
| New brand with no direct reviews | Lower trust | Emphasize people, process, hospitality background, and transparent evidence |
| Package language conflicts with tailor-made positioning | Users expect fixed pricing or booking | Use Journeys or Sample Itineraries and state customization clearly |
| Form becomes too long | High abandonment | Use concise steps, progressive disclosure, and clear rationale |
| Form is too short | Poor lead quality and manual effort | Require essential trip, party, preference, budget, and contact data |
| Manual process becomes overloaded | Slow response and weak experience | Define ownership, service level, tracking, templates, and escalation |
| Tool simplicity hides operational complexity | Lost leads or hidden costs | Validate form, email, consent, scheduling, monitoring, and support workflow |
| Trust assets are ambiguous | Legal and reputational harm | Require explicit attribution and permission |
| English and Greek diverge | Inconsistent or outdated product truth | Establish shared content ownership and localization workflow |
| Too many launch destinations | Thin, repetitive content | Prioritize four strong pages and group secondary destinations |
| Traffic is high but poorly qualified | Operational waste and misleading success | Optimize acquisition and messaging for fit, not volume |
| Premium presentation reduces clarity | Attractive but ineffective experience | Require comprehension and conversion acceptance criteria |
| Premature platform features | Delayed launch and technical debt | Protect MVP scope and require validation for expansion |
| Overreliance on third parties | Vendor lock-in or service failure | Keep lead data structured and operational layers replaceable |

### Key Decisions

- Risks are product concerns when they affect trust, conversion, legal truth, or service delivery.
- Risk mitigation must be reflected in downstream specifications and launch planning.

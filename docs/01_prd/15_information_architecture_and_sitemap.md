## 15. Information Architecture and Sitemap

### 15.1 Top-Level Navigation

- Destinations
- Experiences
- Journeys
- About
- FAQ
- **Plan My Trip**

`Contact` remains a dedicated page and should be prominent in the footer or utility navigation rather than competing in the main navigation.

### 15.3 Naming Rules

Use `Journeys`, `Signature Journeys`, or `Sample Itineraries` instead of `Travel Packages`. Each example must clearly state that it is customizable.

### 15.4 Future Information Architecture

Potential future sections include:

- travel guides and editorial content;
- traveler stories and testimonials;
- gallery and video stories;
- expanded destinations and experiences;
- newsletter, when recurring content exists;
- authenticated client area;
- itinerary management, booking, or payment capabilities if validated.

### Key Decisions

- The main navigation is intentionally small.
- Contact channels support conversion but do not compete with the primary action.
